Mission Statement

At _vTm_, our mission is to help plant enthusiasts and professionals alike to identify, treat, and prevent diseases in their plants. We strive to provide easy-to-use tools and resources that are accessible to everyone. Our ultimate goal is to promote healthy and thriving plants around the world.

Applications
Plant diseases by photo + lunar calendar - Plant Doctor in Your Pocket!

Link in Google Play:
https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.vtm.plantdiseases&pli=1

Description:

The plant disease search by photo is an opportunity to find quickly what your plant is sick, how to cure it and how to prevent the disease in the future. You can enlarge all photos and see the details. The most common plant diseases - collected here!

Click on the name of the disease of the plant in the photo and you will get:
- a detailed description of the plant's disease;
- treatment methods (both folk and with the help of chemicals);
- The list of the most susceptible plants to this disease.
- ways to prevent the disease of your flower, tree, berry, or vegetable;

Bonus: You will receive a lunar sowing calendar for working with your plants at home, in the garden and in the garden. Follow the recommendations and your plants will please you.
